SECTION 3.  There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Administrative Office of the Courts the sum of sixty‑seven thousand six hundred three dollars ($67,603) for the 2019‑2020 fiscal year and one hundred seventy‑three thousand seven hundred ninety‑eight dollars ($173,798) for the 2020‑2021 fiscal year.
