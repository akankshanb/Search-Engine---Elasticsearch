SECTION 2.  There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Administrative Office of the Courts the sum of one hundred nine thousand two hundred twenty‑three dollars ($109,223) in fiscal year 2020‑2021 and one hundred ninety‑eight thousand five hundred eighty‑eight dollars ($198,588) in fiscal year 2021‑2022.

SECTION 3.  Section 1 of this act becomes effective January 1, 2021, and elections conducted in 2020 shall be conducted in accordance with the judgeship created in Section 1. The remainder of this act is effective July 1, 2020.
