A BILL TO BE ENTITLED

AN ACT TO APPOINT PERSONS to various public OFFICES upon the recommendation of the president pro tempore of the senate.

Whereas, G.S. 120‑121 authorizes the General Assembly to make certain appointments to public offices upon the recommendation of the President Pro Tempore of the Senate; and

Whereas, the President Pro Tempore of the Senate has made recommendations; Now, therefore,

The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
