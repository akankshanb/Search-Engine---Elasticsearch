A BILL TO BE ENTITLED

AN ACT to empower employing agencies to administer state short‑term disability benefits, to modernize the medical review board, to address overpayments owed to the DISABILITY income plan by members of the optional retirement program, and to make technical and conforming changes related to these objectives.

The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
