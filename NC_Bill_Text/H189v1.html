A BILL TO BE ENTITLED

AN ACT amending the workers' compensation act to require sole proprietors to maintain workers' compensation insurance.

The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:

SECTION 1.  Article 1 of Chapter 97 is amended by adding a new section to read:

"§ 97‑6.1.  Sole proprietor must maintain workers' compensation insurance

(a)        A sole proprietor performing any work shall maintain a workers' compensation insurance policy in effect at all times. This requirement applies even if the sole proprietor employs no other employees. The sole proprietor is entitled to employee benefits and will be subject to employee responsibilities, as prescribed in this Article.

(b)        If a sole proprietor performs work pursuant to a contract that provides workers' compensation insurance for the sole proprietor, the terms of that contract do not satisfy or supersede the requirements of subsection (a) of this section.

(c)        Failure to comply with the requirements of this section estops the sole proprietor from making a claim on the workers' compensation insurance policy of another person.

(d)       Nothing in this section is intended, or shall be construed, to affect the provisions of G.S. 97‑5.1 relating to the presumption that taxicab drivers are independent contractors."
