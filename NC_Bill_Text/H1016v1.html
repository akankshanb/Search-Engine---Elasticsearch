A BILL TO BE ENTITLED

AN ACT to make appointments to the boards of trustees for CONSTITUENT universities of the university of north carolina and the board of trustees of Cleveland community college.

Whereas, G.S. 116‑31 authorizes the General Assembly to make appointments to the board of trustees of each of the constituent institutions of The University of North Carolina upon the recommendation of the Speaker of the House of Representatives and the President Pro Tempore of the Senate pursuant to G.S. 120‑121; and

Whereas, G.S. 115D‑12, as amended by S.L. 2018‑15, authorizes the General Assembly to make appointments to the Board of Trustees of Cleveland Community College upon the recommendation of the Speaker of the House of Representatives and the President Pro Tempore of the Senate pursuant to G.S. 120‑121; and

Whereas, the Speaker of the House of Representatives and President Pro Tempore of the Senate have made recommendations; Now, therefore,

The General Assembly of North Carolina enacts:
