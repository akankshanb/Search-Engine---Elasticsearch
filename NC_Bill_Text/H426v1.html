SECTION 2.  There is appropriated from the General Fund to the Administrative Office of the Courts the sum of one hundred twenty thousand one hundred twenty‑seven dollars ($120,127) in fiscal year 2020‑2021 and two hundred twenty‑six thousand six hundred ninety‑five dollars ($226,695) in fiscal year 2021‑2022.

SECTION 3.  Section 1 of this act becomes effective January 1, 2021, and elections conducted in 2020 shall be conducted in accordance with the judgeship created in Section 1. The remainder of this act is effective July 1, 2020.
