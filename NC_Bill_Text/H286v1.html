SECTION 1.(b)  If the City of Fayetteville exercises the extraterritorial powers authorized in subsection (a) of this Section, the City shall provide a means of proportional representation on the City's planning board for the residents of Shaw Heights in the manner provided by G.S. 160A‑362.

SECTION 1.(c)  This section becomes effective June 30, 2019.

SECTION 2.  Effective June 30, 2022, the property described in Section 1 of this act is added to the corporate limits of the City of Fayetteville. Property in the territory described in Section 1 of this act as of January 1, 2022, is subject to municipal taxes for taxes imposed for taxable years beginning on or after July 1, 2022.

SECTION 3.  This act is effective when it becomes law.
